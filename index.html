About

Hello there. Business Manager (v1.0.1) was made to help small and medium businesses track and manage well their financial progress. It is a simple yet powerful software package that anyone with little computer literacy can use. Not all features are useful for all business but Business Manager's flexibility allows users to choose what features they want to use. Some of the features include Stock taking, Financial Reports etc..
